about

APIVIS is a modern development company dedicated to creating innovative and secure software solutions. We began with a focus on Node-RED automation, and have since evolved to embrace the power of open-source and the versatility of modern web applications.

Our mission is to build powerful, user-friendly tools that solve real-world problems. We are passionate about sharing our expertise with the community, which is why we've made our secure, embeddable Node-RED solution available as an open-source project.

Alongside our commitment to open-source, we are proud to develop a growing portfolio of Progressive Web Apps (PWAs) and Accelerated Mobile Pages (AMP), designed to deliver exceptional performance and user experience across all devices. From AI-driven legal assistants to smart accounting tools, our applications are built with cutting-edge technology and a focus on quality.
